Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2025

At Luck Wallet, we take your privacy seriously — even though everything on our platform is simulated and no real financial transactions occur. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the limited personal information you provide. By using Luck Wallet, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. What We Collect

We only collect minimal information necessary to provide you with a safe and personalized experience.

Information You Provide:

  • Email address (for login and account creation)
  • Username or display name
  • Optional referral code
  • Optional profile details (avatar, bio)

Information We Generate:

  • Assigned fake wallet address
  • $LUCK balance and transaction history
  • NFT activity and marketplace data
  • Game activity and conversion logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Track your $LUCK balance and activity
  • Enable referral rewards and NFT transactions
  • Improve platform features and gameplay
  • Send you optional platform updates (if you opt in)

We do not:

  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising purposes
  • Track you across other websites

3. Cookies & Analytics

Luck Wallet may use basic cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your dashboard settings
  • Track platform usage statistics (anonymized)

These cookies are purely for user experience and performance improvements.

4. Data Protection & Security

  • Passwords are securely hashed and stored
  • User data is protected with industry-standard practices
  • No sensitive or financial data is collected or stored

As Luck Wallet is a mock platform, all balances, NFTs, and wallet addresses are simulated. However, we still maintain best practices to protect your account and personal info.

5. Children's Privacy

Luck Wallet is intended for students aged 13 and up. If you are under 13, you may not use this platform. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request to view or delete your account data
  • Contact us for any privacy-related questions
  • Opt out of optional communication emails

To request account deletion or data access, email us at: support@luckwallet.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if our platform evolves. We’ll notify users of significant changes via dashboard notice or email (if subscribed).
